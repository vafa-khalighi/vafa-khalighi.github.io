Persian Typesetting in LaTeX: XePersian and other related packages

Slides

Examples

I gave this talk on February 23, 2022 for the department of mathematics and computer science of Sharif University of Technology.

I talked about Persian typesetting in LaTeX, using XePersian and other related packages. The talk was based on version 23.7 of the XePersian package and version 36.7 of the bidi package.

I discussed new features and recent changes in both packages as well as an overview of the kind of things you can do using other related packages.

Some of the examples in the slides are not complete; to see the complete examples, you will need to download examples. Additionally, I tried to give slightly different examples in slides and the accompanying example files.
